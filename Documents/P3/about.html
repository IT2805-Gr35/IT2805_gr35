Norsk Natur for Turister

Mission

Norsk Natur for Turister (NNT) is an organization working to promote and encourage leisurely activity in the Norwegian nature. NNT's goal is to make sure that the nature is accessible to a wider audience. The organization strives to highlight various natural treasures in the Norwegian forest for tourists looking for a unique Norwegian nature experience
